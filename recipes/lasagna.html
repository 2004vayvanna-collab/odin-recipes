Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is rich, cheesy, and comforting. Itâ€™s made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the ground beef with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake until bubbly and golden.
